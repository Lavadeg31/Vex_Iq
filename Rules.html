🤖 VEX IQ Rapid Relay Rules Summary

📜 Key Rules Overview

  • 🔧 Objective of the Game: Players compete on a 6'x6' field aiming to maneuver balls between robots to earn points by scoring goals and activating switches.
  • 🤖 Teamwork Challenge: Two robots collaborate during a 60-second match to pass balls, score goals, and clear switches for maximum points.
  • 🤖 Robot Challenge Task: Robots compete solo in completing tasks, including scoring goals and passing balls, without the help of a partner robot.
  • 🏀 Scoring System Explanation: Points are earned by shooting balls through targets and clearing switches. Each pass between robots also adds points.
  • 🎯 Switches & Targets: There are four switches and four targets. Points are earned by clearing switches and scoring balls through the targets.
  • 🏁 Driver Rotation: In teamwork matches, two drivers must take turns controlling the robot to ensure participation from both drivers during the match.
  • 🔨 Robot Construction: Robots must adhere to size restrictions, use VEX IQ components, and pass inspection before competing. Additional decorations are allowed as long as they do not interfere with gameplay.
  • 🏁 Pre-Match Setup: Robots start in designated areas and may be pre-loaded with a ball for immediate scoring. The field layout and ball positions play a key role in strategy.
  • ⚖️ Penalties: Penalties are applied for violations such as robot mishandling, rule violations regarding field elements, or safety breaches. Severe infractions can lead to disqualification from the match.
  • 🗒️ Final Assessment: After each match, referees calculate the score based on successful passes, switches cleared, and goals scored. The scoring is finalized post-match.
